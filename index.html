About


Hello! My name is Joseph Seo but you can also call me JoJo. I am a recent Computer Science graduate from the University of Iowa looking for full-time software development opportunities. While at Iowa, I involved myself in organizations such as the Association of Computing Machinery, and took on various leadership positions in cultural organizations such as the Asian Pacific American Cultural Center, Pi Alpha Phi Asian Interest Fraternity, and more! I also studied abroad in Norway my final semester which brought me new perspectives both inside and out of the tech space.

Since graduation, I've been doing a lot of self-learning which you can learn more about in my Projects section below. If you have any questions, feel free to click the email icon above and send me a message!

Map My Workout
Map My Workout is a full-stack web application that allows users to log their runs and bike rides with details including location, distance, duration, and more. The application features an interactive map to visualize workout starting locations.

Tech Stack: TypeScript, Node.js, React, Docker

Ad Tracker
Ad Tracker was built using Node.js and Selenium to test and measure the quantity of cookies and scripts a particular browser instance interacts with. I measured these interactions with and without AdBlock Plus to determine the effectiveness of this specific ad blocker. Users may test other ad blockers and websites as well by following the instructions in the README.

Tech Stack: JavaScript, Node.js, Selenium
